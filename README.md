# RTechCoding ERP & CRM Suite

RTechCoding is a comprehensive, AI-powered platform designed to streamline sales workflows, automate lead engagement, and optimize business growth through a centralized, intelligent interface.

## Core Features
* **Lead Lifecycle Management**: Tools for lead discovery, enrichment, and AI-driven scoring based on buying intent.
* **Omnichannel Communication**: Unified inbox for managing customer conversations across multiple channels.
* **AI Sales Intelligence**: AI-powered automation for lead follow-ups, appointment scheduling, and knowledge-based customer support.
* **Performance Analytics**: Real-time insights into sales pipelines, activity feeds, and revenue trends.
* **Customer 360**: Centralized management to track customer interactions and build stronger professional relationships.

## Tech Stack Recommendations

| Component | Recommendation |
| :--- |
| **Frontend Framework** | **Bootstrap 5** (for a responsive, clean, and minimalist corporate aesthetic) |
| **Backend Language** | **PHP** (custom development for dynamic product/lead management and admin panels) |
| **UI Design Patterns** | **Glassmorphism** effects, **Z-pattern** layouts, and spacious, white-background minimalism |
| **Security & Integration** | **hCaptcha** for form validation and AJAX-based submission handlers |
| **AI Integration** | **Unison AI CRM Suite** for automated ERP workflows and CRM intelligence |

## Project Status
* Currently in development, featuring active modules for "RTechPulse" newsletters, ROI calculators, and dynamic CRM tools.
